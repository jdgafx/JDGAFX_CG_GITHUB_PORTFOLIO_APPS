About

Who I Am

I'm Chris Gentile — a generative AI and hyperagentic engineer who ships production systems, not prototypes. I build with Claude Code, the Anthropic API, and the latest Claude models (Haiku 4.5, Sonnet 4.6, Opus 4.6) to create multi-agent systems that reason, collaborate, and deliver results autonomously.

My stack: React 19, TypeScript 5, Vite 6, Tailwind v4, Netlify Functions, Supabase, SSE streaming, and agentic AI orchestration. Every app below is live, deployed, and handling real requests on serverless infrastructure.

I practice hyperagentic development — where AI agents don't just assist, they architect, code, test, and deploy. The ten applications below are the proof.
